About Poll Portal:

Poll Portal is a site made for debating various topics. It was designed so that both sides can share their opinions in a way that prevents arguing back and forth. Arguing back and forth turns people away from healthy debates. The goal of Poll Portal was to allow all sides to say their opinions in a way that prevents arguments by seperating the comments for each opinion.
